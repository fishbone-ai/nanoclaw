Initiative Overview
AI Visibility Initiative

Shoppers increasingly start their search in AI assistants rather than traditional search engines. When someone asks "what's a good gift for a home decor lover under £100?", AI systems surface products based on the quality and completeness of their catalog data - attributes like color, material, product type, and structured descriptions.

This initiative is about understanding what moves the needle for Knots Studio specifically, and doing it in a way we can measure clearly.

Baseline Assessment
Where Things Stand Today

We pulled your full Google Merchant Center catalog on April 1, 2026. Here's what we found across the fields AI systems use to match products to queries:

Field Status
Color Not populated for most products; where present, values contain SKU codes rather than color names
Product type Not populated for most products
Structured attributes Material, dimensions, and similar fields not populated
Brand Inconsistent formatting across products
Product highlights Not set
The Work
What We're Doing

We've selected 38 products for this initiative. For each, we're enriching the key attributes Google uses for AI matching - color, product type, brand, structured taxonomy fields, descriptions, and product highlights.

Changes are made directly in Shopify, which syncs to Google Merchant Center automatically. Prices, inventory, storefront, and campaign settings are untouched.
