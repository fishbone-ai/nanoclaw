# פיט בוט — Ad Creative Brief (Phase 0.2)

**Purpose:** 6 Hebrew Meta creatives for the Click-to-WhatsApp smoke test described in [TODO.md §0.2–§1.1](TODO.md).
**Scope:** 2 angles × 3 formats = 6 creatives total.
**Audience:** Israel, Hebrew, 22–40, Advantage+ targeting.
**Budget:** within the ₪700 CBO over 7 days (see [TODO.md §1.1](TODO.md)).

## Strategic choices (locked)

| Dimension | Choice | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Angle A | **Pain — restart cycle + overwhelm.** "3 apps downloaded, opened each twice; every influencer says the opposite." | Addresses the specific failure loop the target audience actually lives in. Specificity beats generic "struggling to get fit" framing. |
| Angle B | **Friend — in-WhatsApp + photo-and-done.** "It's in the app you already open 100× a day; snap a meal, get an answer." | Leads with the single genuine product differentiator vs. fitness apps in Israel: WhatsApp-native. Demo is shootable. |
| Pronoun form | **Plural ("אתם") throughout all ads.** | Matches M1 opener exactly so ad voice → WhatsApp voice is seamless. Aligns with SPEC.md §7 "plural until gender known" rule. |
| Em dashes | **None.** | Per project convention (commit 0e817de). Use commas, periods, or short sentences. |
| Face-to-camera | **No face in any creative.** Voice + hands + phone screen only. | Operator averse to face UGC; "faceless UGC + founder voice" retains most of the authenticity lift without the cringe cost. Voice carries Hebrew tonal texture that text overlays cannot. |
| Production effort | **Laddered by format:** 9:16 gets voice + hands, 4:5 gets screen-record + overlays only, 1:1 is text-forward design. | Concentrates effort on the format that carries most spend (9:16 vertical). Lower-effort formats exist to test the "pure demo" and "static fallback" hypotheses at low cost. |

## Research basis for the no-face + voice + hands choice

Directional evidence, not proof. The ₪700 / 6-creative test is not statistically powered to detect modest lifts anyway; this choice is about production practicality given the operator.

- Industry consensus: UGC-style ~4× engagement vs. polished brand content; founder-led ~30–60% higher engagement. Primary-source traceability is thin for the widely cited "+40% CTR from face" figure; treat as folk wisdom.
- Strong counter-signal from Motion's 2026 benchmarks (analyzed $1.3B in spend): text-forward assets (text-only, product + text overlays, simple GIFs) "frequently rank well." Pure screen-record is not a creative dead end.
- Founder-video advantage is conditional on *authenticity*, not polish. Forced awkward face-UGC forfeits the lift. Voice + hands retains most of the "human is here" signal without face-camera risk.

Sources: [Nord Media on founder videos](https://subscribe.nordmedia.com/p/ugly-founder-videos-are-beating-your-polished-ugc), [Motion 2026 Creative Benchmarks](https://motionapp.com/thumbstop-pulse/creative-benchmarks-2026), [Expanse Digital on UGC](https://www.expansedigital.co/post/why-ugc-ads-outperform-professional-ads-and-how-to-create-them-in-2026).

**Primary text:**


**CTA button:** `שלח הודעה` (Meta CTW default)

## Production checklist

**Tools required:**
- One phone (the WhatsApp Business Fitbot number's phone is fine).
- A second phone or device to send the "bot" replies during recording.
- Native iOS/Android screen recording.
- Any basic video editor for trimming, overlay text, end-card: CapCut (free, mobile), DaVinci Resolve (free, desktop), or iMovie.
- Hebrew font: Heebo or Assistant (free via Google Fonts).

**Before shooting:**
- [ ] Script the "bot" replies (write down exact Hebrew text to paste from the sender phone during screen-recording). Messages must be short, natural, match the system prompt tone.
- [ ] Prepare 1 real plate of food (one angle needs Israeli-legible food: shakshuka, burekas, humus + pita, or similar).
- [ ] Clear the phone home screen so fitness app icons are visible and prominent for Angle A.
- [ ] Full phone battery, airplane mode (no notifications during recording), max brightness.

**Voiceover (Angle A + B 9:16 only):**
- [ ] Record in a quiet room. AirPods mic is fine. Aim for ~6–8 short lines, each under 3 seconds.
- [ ] Record takes separately per line. Pick the best per line, comp them in post.
- [ ] Tone: casual Israeli gym-buddy, low volume, conversational. No voice-actor delivery.

**Hook test (per creative):**
- [ ] Play first 2 seconds with sound off on a phone at native size. Is the product + promise clear? If no, reshoot the opening.

**Mobile preview:**
- [ ] View every final export on an actual phone at native size before submitting to Meta. Do not judge from desktop.
