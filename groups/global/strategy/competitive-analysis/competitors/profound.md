## Overview

Enterprise AI brand visibility and monitoring platform. Tracks how brands and products appear across AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Grok) and provides tools to optimize AI-generated representation. Highest-funded company in the Catalog & Content layer of the agentic commerce ecosystem.

**Company:** Profound
**HQ:** USA
**Founded:** ~2023
**Platform:** Platform-agnostic (web/API monitoring)
**Stage:** Series C ($96M, $1B valuation)

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed. Enterprise contracts. Clients include Target, Walmart, Indeed, MongoDB, Figma.

## What they do

- AI search monitoring — tracks brand/product mentions across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Grok, Google AI Overviews
- Share-of-voice analytics for AI-generated answers
- "Profound Agents" for automated marketing orchestration
- Proprietary AI visibility scoring and citation analytics
- Recommendations for improving AI representation of products/brands
- Competitive benchmarking in AI search results

## Strengths

- Largest funding in this category ($96M Series C, $1B valuation)
- Blue-chip enterprise customer list (Target, Walmart, Figma, MongoDB, Indeed)
- Strong brand and thought leadership in the GEO/AEO category
- "Profound Agents" for automated optimization shows platform ambition
- Distribution advantage via enterprise sales motion

## Weaknesses

- Not catalog enrichment — monitors and optimizes how existing content appears, doesn't generate or restructure it
- Enterprise-only pricing likely puts them out of reach for mid-market
- GEO/monitoring category may commoditize as platforms (Adobe, Semrush) add similar features
- Heavy enterprise sales cycle; slow to ship new features

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Adjacent** — Different tooling today: Profound monitors AI visibility, Fishbone enriches the underlying catalog data. But their "AI Commerce" framing and the implicit dependency on clean catalog data upstream makes them a natural partner rather than competitor. Long-term convergence risk if they build enrichment features.
