## Overview

Berlin-based AI search marketing / GEO platform. Helps brands track, analyze, and improve how they appear in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini). Founded February 2025 via Antler Berlin. Exceptional growth trajectory: $4M+ ARR in 10 months, 1,300+ brands.

**Company:** Peec AI
**HQ:** Berlin, Germany
**Founded:** February 2025
**Platform:** Platform-agnostic (web-based analytics)
**Stage:** Series A ($21M led by Singular, ~$29M total)

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed. Mid-market and agency tiers available.

## What they do

- AI search monitoring — tracks brand mentions, rankings, and citation rates across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Grok, and others
- GEO analytics — identifies what content drives AI mentions and recommendations
- Competitor benchmarking in AI search results
- Content optimization recommendations to improve AI discoverability
- Agency-friendly reporting and white-labeling
- Expanding toward full "AI-era marketing stack"

## Strengths

- Hypergrowth — $4M+ ARR in 10 months is exceptional for a B2B SaaS
- Series A at strong valuation with tier-1 investor (Singular)
- Strong European market presence (Axel Springer, Chanel, TUI, ElevenLabs as clients)
- Agency distribution channel (multiplies reach without direct sales)
- Speed of execution — shipped fast, scaled fast

## Weaknesses

- GEO analytics, not catalog enrichment — different layer
- European-first may limit US mid-market reach short-term
- Growing competitive pressure from Profound, Adobe, Semrush all moving into GEO
- Platform breadth vs. depth tradeoff as they expand the "marketing stack"

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Adjacent** — GEO/AI visibility analytics rather than catalog enrichment. However, their core insight — that rich, structured product data drives AI discoverability — is the same thesis Fishbone operates from. Partnership angle: Fishbone enriches the catalog, Peec demonstrates the resulting AI visibility lift.
