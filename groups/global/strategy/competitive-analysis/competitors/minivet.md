## Overview

GenAI startup that transforms static product catalogs into dynamic video content at scale for e-commerce. Converts product listings into video stories, enabling sellers to publish millions of polished videos at a fraction of traditional production cost. Also offers conversational search, semantic frameworks, and image/video enhancement tools. **Acquired by Flipkart (India's largest e-commerce platform) in December 2025** — majority stake.

**Company:** Minivet AI
**HQ:** India
**Founded:** 2024
**Platform:** E-commerce integrations (Flipkart-native post-acquisition)
**Stage:** Acquired by Flipkart (Dec 2025); pre-acquisition funding undisclosed

## Pricing

No longer independently available — Flipkart-owned.

## What they do (pre-acquisition)

- Generative video from product catalog listings — static images → video stories at scale
- AI image and video enhancement (background removal, 4K upscaling)
- Conversational search and semantic frameworks for product discovery
- Contextual intelligence for personalized, media-rich catalog experiences
- Per-merchant model orchestration for brand-safe output

## Strengths

- Acquired by Flipkart — validated technology and team quality
- Video-first catalog content is a differentiated wedge — most catalog tools focus on text/attributes
- Flipkart gives massive distribution and compute resources

## Weaknesses

- Now Flipkart-owned — not an independent competitor for most markets
- India/South Asia focus post-acquisition limits global competitive relevance
- Video generation is a catalog content play, not structured attribute enrichment

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Minimal (post-acquisition)** — As a Flipkart-owned asset focused on video catalog content for the Indian market, this is not a competitive concern for Fishbone's current focus. The acquisition signals that catalog content generation (even in video form) is valued enough for a major platform to buy rather than build — another category validation signal.
