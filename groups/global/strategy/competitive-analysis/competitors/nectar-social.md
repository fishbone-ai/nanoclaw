## Overview

AI platform that transforms organic social channels into revenue engines for DTC brands. Provides AI-powered community management, social listening, influencer tracking, and full-funnel revenue attribution for social commerce. Not a catalog enrichment tool — included in the Agentic Commerce Ecosystem Map under Catalog & Content but operates in social commerce.

**Company:** Nectar Social
**HQ:** USA
**Founded:** Mid-2023
**Platform:** Instagram, TikTok, other social platforms
**Stage:** Seed ($10.6M led by True Ventures and GV/Google Ventures, June 2025)

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed.

## What they do

- AI-powered community management trained on brand tone and customer history
- Social listening and engagement analytics
- Influencer tracking and performance attribution
- Full-funnel revenue attribution from organic social interactions
- Personalized response generation for social comments/DMs
- Revenue attribution linking social engagement to purchases

## Strengths

- Strong investors (True Ventures + GV/Google Ventures)
- Clear DTC/CPG customer focus with named brands (OLIPOP, Jones Road Beauty, Solawave)
- Revenue attribution in social commerce is a real pain point
- Not competing in catalog enrichment — complements it

## Weaknesses

- Different domain from catalog enrichment
- Social commerce is a crowded space (Attentive, Braze, etc.)
- Attribution in social remains methodologically contentious

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Minimal** — Social commerce is a distinct domain from catalog enrichment. No meaningful overlap. Possible complement: enriched catalog data can power better social commerce content and product tagging, but not a competitive consideration.
