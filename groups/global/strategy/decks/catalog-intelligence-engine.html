01
Diagnostic Loop
Query Bank fires against the raw catalog. Failures traced to specific attribute gaps produce two remediation signals: fix existing, add missing.
02
Remediation Loop
Track A normalizes and cleans. Track B adds net-new attributes. Both produce per-value traceability and confidence scores. No black-box values.
03
Monitoring Loop
Enriched catalog continuously re-tested per platform. Drift detected, root causes traced, re-enrichment triggered automatically. The loop never ends.
