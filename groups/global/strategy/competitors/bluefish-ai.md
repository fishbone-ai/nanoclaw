Last updated: 2026-03-10

## Overview

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) platform for Fortune 500 brands. Monitors and optimizes how brands appear across generative AI platforms. Emerged from stealth December 2024. Has an "AI Commerce" module targeting product selection in agentic shopping flows — the piece closest to Fishbone's territory.

**Company:** Bluefish AI
**HQ:** USA
**Founded:** 2023
**Platform:** Platform-agnostic (web/API)
**Stage:** Series A ($20M led by NEA, ~$24M total)

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed. Enterprise-focused.

## What they do

- GEO monitoring — tracks brand/product presence in AI-generated answers
- Proprietary metrics: Impact Score, Influence Rank
- "AI Commerce" module — optimizes product data for selection by AI shopping agents
- Competitive benchmarking across AI platforms
- Automated content recommendations to improve AI representation
- Strong adtech pedigree (founders from PromoteIQ/Microsoft, LiveRail/Facebook)

## Strengths

- Strong Series A with credible investors (NEA, Salesforce Ventures, Bloomberg Beta, Crane)
- Founder DNA from adtech at scale (PromoteIQ, LiveRail)
- "AI Commerce" module is drifting into product data optimization territory
- Enterprise brand clients (Adidas, Tishman Speyer)
- Clear monetization path from existing adtech sales playbooks

## Weaknesses

- GEO monitoring at core, not catalog enrichment — different value prop
- Enterprise-only, unlikely to serve mid-market
- "AI Commerce" module is early/nascent; not yet a deep catalog enrichment product
- Crowded GEO monitoring space (Profound, Peec AI, Adobe all competing)

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Adjacent** — Their "AI Commerce" module is the piece to watch; if they build out structured product data enrichment, they converge on Fishbone's lane. Today, they're upstream visibility, Fishbone is upstream data quality. Partnership angle: Fishbone enriches → Bluefish measures the impact.
