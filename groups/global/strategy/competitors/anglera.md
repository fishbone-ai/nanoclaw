## Overview

YC-backed AI platform for product data enrichment, onboarding, and listing at scale. Processes product data from spreadsheets, PDFs, and websites to onboard new SKUs in seconds rather than weeks. Officially launched August 2025. Founders' background at Uber Eats (enriching millions of restaurant/product listings) is the operational DNA behind the product.

**Company:** ANGLERA
**HQ:** USA
**Founded:** 2024/2025
**Platform:** Multi-channel (API-based, marketplace integrations)
**Stage:** Seed ($500K, YC W25 batch)

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed.

## What they do

- AI-powered product data extraction from spreadsheets, PDFs, supplier websites
- Structured attribute enrichment and standardization
- Rapid SKU onboarding (seconds vs. weeks claim)
- Marketplace-ready listing generation
- "Agentic commerce infrastructure" positioning — catalog enrichment as the foundation for AI agent discoverability
- Targets brands, retailers, and marketplaces

## Strengths

- YC backing — strongest signal for early-stage credibility and investor pipeline
- Founder DNA from Uber Eats marketplace data operations — hands-on experience with high-volume catalog enrichment at scale
- "Agentic commerce infrastructure" narrative is well-positioned for the current moment
- Fast-moving early-stage team

## Weaknesses

- Very early stage — $500K seed, limited traction evidence
- Small team and resource base
- YC alumni network could help or hurt (creates awareness but also investor competition with other YC companies)
- No published customer case studies or metrics yet

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Direct** — Nearly identical proposition to Fishbone: AI-powered product data enrichment for catalog readiness and AI agent discoverability. Both are early-stage and targeting the same market. Most likely to compete for the same funding conversations and early customer deals. This is the closest like-for-like competitor.
