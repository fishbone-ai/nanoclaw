## Overview

Google organic Shopping rank tracker and supplemental feed optimizer. ProductRise shows merchants where their products rank in Google's organic product carousels (which GMC doesn't expose), tracks competitor positions, and generates supplemental feeds with optimized data to improve those rankings.

**HQ:** Unknown
**Founded:** Unknown
**Target:** E-commerce merchants and agencies optimizing for Google organic Shopping visibility
**Scale:** Unknown (free tier available, no credit card required)

## What they do

### Rank Tracking (core)
- Track organic product carousel positions across Google Search (GMC doesn't show this data)
- Competitor position tracking -- see where rivals rank for the same keywords
- Multi-country, multi-language tracking (every country and language)
- Measures pixel position, page depth, and weighted position score across desktop and mobile
- Initial insights within minutes of connecting feed; ranking improvements visible in 2-4 weeks

### Supplemental Feed Generation
- Generate supplemental feeds with optimized product data (titles, attributes, etc.)
- Add public feed URL to GMC -- no developer required
- Optimizations scoped to organic "Free listings" only -- doesn't touch paid campaigns
- Can help merchants create their first feed from scratch (manual or website import)

### Agency / Multi-tenant
- Multi-tenant: invite team members or clients, share access at property level
- Create separate properties per brand/website under one account

## How they compare to us

ProductRise is squarely in the traditional Google organic Shopping world. Their value prop is "GMC doesn't show you rank data -- we do." No AI agent / GEO / agentic commerce angle visible on the site.

**Overlap:**
- Supplemental feed generation -- same technical mechanism we're using for Knots Studio
- GMC-adjacent tooling -- same ecosystem
- Feed optimization at the product data level

**Differences:**
- Purely focused on Google organic Shopping rank -- no ChatGPT, AI agent, or GEO angle
- Rank tracking is their core product; Fishbone doesn't do rank tracking
- Self-serve SaaS, free tier, marketer-facing (no-code)
- No catalog enrichment depth (attributes, AI agent readiness, structured data)
- Their supplemental feed is optimized for Google Shopping signals, not AI agent queries

**Positioning:** A rank-tracking + feed-optimization tool for traditional Google Shopping. Relevant to merchants who care about organic Shopping positions. Not a threat on the ChatGPT App / AI discovery front.

## Strengths

- Clear differentiation from GMC (fills a genuine data gap GMC doesn't provide)
- No credit card required -- frictionless free tier
- Marketer-friendly, no developer needed
- Multi-country and agency-ready out of the box
- Clean positioning ("think of us as a powerful layer that GMC is missing")

## Weaknesses

- No AI agent / agentic commerce positioning
- Rank tracking is Google-only -- no coverage of AI search surfaces (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.)
- Feed optimization appears to be for traditional Shopping signals, not AI-readiness signals
- No catalog enrichment depth (attribute generation, taxonomy, structured AI data)

## Assessment

Adjacent competitor, not a direct threat to Fishbone's ChatGPT App / AI discovery focus. Overlap exists on the GMC supplemental feed side (same mechanism we're using with Knots Studio). If Fishbone expands to include traditional Google Shopping rank tracking, ProductRise becomes more relevant. For now, monitor as an adjacent player -- they're optimizing for Google organic Shopping, we're optimizing for AI agent discovery.

**Threat level:** Low (adjacent)
