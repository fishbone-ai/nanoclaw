# Novi

Last updated: 2026-03-10

## Links

## Overview

AI Shopping Optimizer that optimizes product data and trust signals (badges, certifications, retailer endorsements) to increase product visibility and selection rates in AI-driven shopping (Google Gemini, ChatGPT Shopping, etc.). Frames their work as "AEO" (AI-driven E-commerce Optimization). Launched AI Shopping Optimizer September 2025.

**Company:** Novi Connect
**HQ:** USA
**Founded:** ~2022 (AI Shopping Optimizer launched Sep 2025)
**Platform:** Platform-agnostic (retailer connectivity layer)
**Stage:** Seed (Defy.vc backed; funding amount undisclosed)

## Pricing

Not publicly disclosed.

## What they do

- Product data optimization for AI shopping agents (structured attributes, descriptions)
- Trust signal management — badges, certifications, retailer endorsements
- AI product selection rate optimization — claims 230%+ lift in AI product selection
- Retailer connectivity (Target, Ulta, Sephora referenced as distribution partners)
- "AEO" (AI-driven E-commerce Optimization) positioning
- Ensures verified, consistent product data across AI shopping surfaces

## Strengths

- Unique "trust signals + product data" combination — not just enrichment but credentialing
- Strong retailer network (Target, Ulta, Sephora as distribution partners)
- Backed by Defy.vc (tier-1 early-stage investor)
- 230%+ AI product selection lift claim is compelling if substantiated
- Retailer connectivity is a hard-to-replicate distribution advantage

## Weaknesses

- Trust signals/certification focus is a different motion than core catalog enrichment
- Retailer partnership model may be slow to scale
- "AEO" framing overlaps with several other companies making similar claims
- Limited public information about product depth and actual enrichment capabilities

## Threat level to Fishbone

**Adjacent** — Novi operates at the intersection of catalog data optimization and trust/credentialing for AI agents. Their "product data for AI agents" framing is conceptually close to Fishbone's. The trust signals differentiation means they're not a pure catalog enrichment play. Worth monitoring for convergence.
